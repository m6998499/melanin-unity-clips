Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

Melanin Unity Clips is a video discovery and creator submission app. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how users can request deletion.

Information We Collect

  • Email address submitted with a video upload.
  • Uploaded videos, titles, captions, categories, file metadata, and moderation status.
  • Comments submitted on published videos, including display name and comment text.
  • Technical data needed to operate the app, such as basic device, browser, storage, and error information.

How We Use Information

  • To receive, store, review, approve, reject, publish, or remove user-submitted videos.
  • To display approved community clips and comments.
  • To moderate content, prevent abuse, respond to support requests, and improve app reliability.
  • To comply with legal, security, app store, and safety requirements.

User-Generated Content

Videos and comments submitted by users may be reviewed before or after publication. We may reject, remove, or restrict content that violates our guidelines, app store rules, legal requirements, or user safety standards.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We use service providers such as hosting, storage, database, and app distribution platforms to operate the app. These providers process information only as needed to provide the service.

Retention

We keep uploaded videos, comments, moderation records, and related metadata for as long as needed to operate the app, maintain safety, resolve disputes, comply with legal obligations, or support deletion requests.

Deletion Requests

Users can request removal of uploaded videos, comments, or associated email information by contacting support. Some security, moderation, or legal records may be retained when necessary.

Children

Melanin Unity Clips is not intended for children under 13. Users should only submit content they have the right to upload and share.

Contact

For privacy, support, or deletion requests, email: support@melaninunityclips.com
