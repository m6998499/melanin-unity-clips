Upload rules

  • Accepted types: MP4, MOV, and WebM.
  • Recommended format: 9:16 vertical, 720p or higher.
  • Current maximum size: 250 MB.
  • All public uploads default to pending_review.
  • Admins review, approve, reject, or remove videos in Supabase.

App store submission hub

Metadata

Short description: A scroll-only video discovery app for creators, ideas, culture, lifestyle, and community-driven clips.

Apple keywords: short video, clips, creator, community, lifestyle, culture, motivation, education, wellness, local business.

Categories and rating

Apple primary: Entertainment. Secondary: Lifestyle or Social Networking. Google Play primary: Entertainment. Age guidance: Teen/12+ because user-uploaded content and moderation are present.

Submission checklist

  • Apple: metadata, privacy labels, age rating, screenshots, demo account, support URL, privacy URL, signed iOS build.
  • Google: store listing, data safety form, content rating, screenshots, privacy URL, signed Android App Bundle.
  • Both: replace placeholder emails, connect production Supabase, verify account deletion, test moderation workflow.
